# Getting Started with NereusSDR

NereusSDR is under active development. This guide will be updated as features are implemented.

## Requirements

- An OpenHPSDR Protocol 1 or Protocol 2 compatible radio
- Network connection to the radio (Ethernet)

## Connecting to Your Radio

1. Launch NereusSDR
2. Go to Radio > Discover to find radios on your network
3. Select your radio and click Connect

## Supported Radios

- Apache Labs ANAN line (ANAN-G2, ANAN-7000DLE, ANAN-8000DLE, ANAN-200D, etc.)
- Hermes Lite 2
- All OpenHPSDR Protocol 1 and Protocol 2 compatible radios
